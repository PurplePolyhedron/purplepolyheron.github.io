Purple Slime Counter

Purple Slime Counter is a resources tracker for boardgames. Current version is optimazed for a Commander game. There are plans to add general board game support later.

Coming soon to Google Play and Apple App Store.

You can also try the web version, or check the Github(Getting ready)

Commander damage

Deals Commander damage with a single gesture.
Simple and fast, and it supports partner commander

Supports up to six player

Each section can be rotated individually

Add more counter

You can additional counter stats. Or add a floating counter to indicate initiative

History keeping

Keeps track of all the life total. Turn on history retrace the game history when needed.

Highly Customizable

You can pick the avatars or backgrounds you like. And use your own images and assets
